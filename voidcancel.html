Void Cancel Complete Guide

Master the glitch that allows you to fall forever and bypass all limits

Method 1: Cutscene Skip Method (Recommended)

This is the primary method used in Any% speedruns. It requires performing a Cutscene Skip first.

Perform the Cutscene Skip

From the main menu, choose new game > difficulty and when you validate, quickly press: Down > Enter > Hold stick forward > Dash > Jump > Attack. You should hear these actions during the black-screen.

Fall Until Respawn Attempt

After the cutscene skip, fall into the void. The game will try to respawn you but will fail due to the CS state.

Continuous Fall Damage

Instead of respawning, you'll continuously receive fall damage. This is the sign that the glitch is activating.

Menu Navigation

While taking damage, go back to the main menu and select "Continue".

Void Cancel Activated!

You can now fall forever with no void and no respawn. The glitch is permanently attached to your save file.

Tips & Tricks

  • Sound ON: Keep the game sound on to hear your inputs during the cutscene
  • Controller vibration: You should feel a vibration after the attack input - good sign!
  • Success indicators: One doesn't talk after the cutscene and there's no camera movement
  • Practice: Try different timings and find what suits your playstyle

Method 2: Death Method (No CS Required)

Alternative method that doesn't require the Cutscene Skip. Useful for practice or specific situations.

Lose Most of Your HP

Take damage until you have almost no HP left. This is faster in Hard difficulty as enemies deal more damage.

Fall to Your Death

Jump into the void and let yourself fall. You should die from fall damage.

Pause Buffer Timing

CRITICAL STEP: You need to pause buffer to aim the first frames of the death animation. This means taking the last hit just after being respawned.

Force the Start Menu

After the respawn, quickly try to force the start menu. You may struggle with this - press "Resume" like shown in the video tutorial.

Controller Vibration Check

Sometimes the controller will be vibrating on this frame and continuously goes on when you're on the pause menu. This is a good sign but doesn't seem mandatory.

Main Menu > Continue

Go back to the main menu and select "Continue". You should now have the ability to fall forever.

Important Notes

  • This method is more difficult than the CS method
  • Requires precise frame-perfect timing
  • Good for practicing without resetting your entire run
  • Works on any difficulty (easier in Hard mode)

Warnings

  • Fatal Error Risk: Don't fall for too long or you'll get a crash
  • No Going Back: Once activated, you'll have to continue without ever voiding again
  • Save File: The glitch is stuck to your save - you can't disable it
  • Continuous Damage: You'll take fall damage continuously (manage your HP!)
